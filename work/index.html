Selected work

A small body of considered work.

Every project below was hand-coded from scratch and shaped around one specific practice — its clients, its specialties, its voice. No shared template. No platform fees. Each client owns their domain, hosting, and code outright.

fullbloomcounseling.com
Group practice

Full Bloom Counseling Group

Denver, Colorado

A growing Denver group practice of seven clinicians needed a website that could hold every therapist's story without collapsing into a faceless directory. The goal was a site that felt like the practice itself — warm, credible, and easy for the right client to navigate to the right person.

What I built: individual clinician profiles, specialty service pages, JSON-LD structured data for local search, analytics, and a contact form with multiple delivery fallbacks so no inquiry ever gets lost. Hand-coded and deployed on edge hosting at no monthly cost.

7 clinician profiles Specialty service pages Structured data Analytics $0 hosting

lovettcounselingandconsulting.com
Solo practice

Lovett Counseling & Consulting

Denver, Colorado

A trauma-informed counselor (LPC, CCTP) building a practice around depth and pacing needed a site that read the way her sessions feel — unhurried, warm, and honest. The writing mattered as much as the design.

What I built: a full multi-page site — separate pages for individual therapy, couples therapy, and consulting & training, plus a fees page, an FAQ, and an about page — each with its own structured data and local SEO, wrapped in an editorial voice built to turn a quiet reader into a first phone call.

Multi-page build Individual & couples Consulting pages Fees & FAQ Trauma-informed voice

safersoundsclub.com
Specialty brand & digital product

The Safer Sounds Club

Online workshop

Not every therapist project is a private-practice homepage. This one is a specialty brand and digital workshop for a licensed therapist helping people with misophonia through somatic work — a distinct identity that needed to feel like a warm invitation, not a clinic.

What I built: a narrative landing page, an interactive self-assessment quiz, workshop and blog sections, and email capture to grow a waitlist — all hand-coded with its own voice and design language, entirely separate from any therapy-directory template.
