Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Quick answers before we talk. Still have questions? The contact form is right below.

How much does a therapist website cost?

+
Packages start at $499 for a clean, professional 4-page site. Most solo therapists choose the Growth package at $899 — it includes full SEO, custom design, and copywriting support. Group practices start at $1,499. All pricing is one-time; you'll never pay a monthly platform fee to me.

Do I own the website after it's built?

+
Yes — completely. Your domain, your hosting account, your WordPress login. Everything is in your name. You're never locked into working with me, and the site doesn't disappear if we stop working together. This is a key difference from platforms like Brighter Vision or TherapySites.

How long does it take?

+
Most sites launch within 2–3 weeks of receiving your deposit and completed intake questionnaire. The timeline depends mostly on how quickly you can provide feedback — I keep things moving on my end and send progress updates every few days.

Do I have to write my own copy?

+
No. Copywriting support is available starting at $150. I'll ask you the right questions, then write your homepage, about page, and service descriptions in your voice. Most clients say it's their favorite part of the process — and it takes a task off your plate that most therapists dread.

Will my site show up on Google?

+
Every site I build is optimized for search from day one — meta tags, site structure, XML sitemap, and Rank Math SEO configured for your location and specialty. Realistic timeline for organic results is 3–6 months. For faster local visibility, I also offer Google Business Profile setup for $75.

Do you work with practices outside Colorado?

+
Yes — I work with therapists and private practice counselors nationwide. Everything is handled remotely. The process is identical whether you're in Denver or Miami.

Do you only build therapy websites?

+
Therapists are my specialty and where I do my best work — but no, I'm not limited to it. I build WordPress websites for other wellness professionals as well — coaches, nutritionists, acupuncturists, and small-practice service providers. For creative projects, custom web apps, or work that calls for something more experimental, I also run a separate studio: studionull.io. Reach out either way and I'll point you in the right direction.

I already have a website — can you update or improve it?

+
Absolutely. If your existing site is built on WordPress, I can make edits, refresh the design, add pages, fix broken elements, or improve your SEO — all billed at $100/hour with no minimum commitment. Not on WordPress? Get in touch anyway and I'll let you know what's possible.
